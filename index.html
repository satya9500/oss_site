About Us

We are the Open Source Software Club of AIT. We are involved in developing open source softwares for the benefit of our college and it's students. We organise great events to encourage open source such as Innerve and AWOC. Innerve is a nation wide Hackathon organised by our club.

Our recent events

Innerve

Innerve is the annual hackathon of Army Institute Of Technology, Pune. This is the biggest student organised hackathon in Pune, where teams from all over the country participates.

Graphica

Graphica is a event specially organised for First years to introduce them to the open source technologies.

AIT Winter Of Code

AIT Winter Of Code is an event that introduces students to open source contribution and GSoC

Our recent Classes/Topics

Front-End Development
